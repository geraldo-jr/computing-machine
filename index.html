The Hyperloop technology is a futuristic vision for a transportation system of magnetically levitating pods traveling through nearly airless tubes at speeds of up to 760 mph (1,223 km/h). First public conceived by Tesla and SpaceX CEO Elon Musk and now applied in different projects around the globe by different companies and universities.
